Headlines and summaries are pulled from each publisher's RSS feed periodically, not on page load, and shown unedited except for truncation where necessary. Each source is limited to a recent time window and item count. Source limits and display orders are defined in the config file. Feed update rate is defined in the deploy file.

Headlines you open are remembered only in your browser (via localStorage), and forgotten when the feed refreshes.
